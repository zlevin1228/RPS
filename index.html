How to Play

Press the 'Start Game!' button to begin. A prompt will pop up and ask you to type in a choice. You can choose from either ROCK, PAPER, SCISSORS, or a secret option that will guarantee you a win. The computer will display your choice and make its own choice. ROCK beats SCISSORS, SCISSORS beats PAPER, and PAPER beats ROCK. You will either win, tie, or lose. Your wins and the computer's wins will be displayed in the scoreboard below. The first one to 5 round wins is the winner!
